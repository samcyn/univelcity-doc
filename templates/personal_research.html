Developing User personas and Research Methods

About this Course
Step into the world of user experience design. During the class we will explore; the decision-making process that dictates how users interact with web products, research methods for understanding users and developing user personas. All this will culminate into a fundamental understanding of how to develop products that better serve customer and add value to them.
Takeaways
  • Learn what the world of UX design encompasses and why it matters
  • Touch on fundamental tools and techniques used by professional designers
  • Practice interviewing techniques to gain user insights
  • Discover resources to continue learning about UX
Prerequirements and Preparations
  • Students should come to class with a laptop
  • Some understanding of User Interface Design (UI) would be beneficial but is not essential
