Android and iOS Mobile Application Design

About this Course
Learn how to design mobile applications for both android and iOS platforms, two of the most popular mobile operating systems in the world. The class will look at everything from Material Design which is an android guideline for designing android mobile applications to Human Interface Design which is an iOS guideline for designing mobile applications. The designs you come up with will be analysed by us as a group so we can all get better together.
Takeaways
  • Understanding of Material Design Guidelines
  • Understanding of Human Interface Guidelines
  • A design for an Android mobile application
  • A design for an iOS mobile application
Prerequirements and Preparations
    Students must come to class with a laptop with the following pre-installed:
  • a free trial for Sketch 3 from https://www.sketchapp.com/
  • a free trial for Adobe XD from http://www.adobe.com/africa/products/experience-design/features.html
